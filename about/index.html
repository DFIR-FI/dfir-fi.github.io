About

This website has been developed purely to meet the need to list DFIR providers in Finland. Juho has previously published blog posts on another site, and 2024 decided to merge the pages into one larger entity. On this site you can also find course offerings for DFIR work in Finland. In addition to the website, DFIR-FI is a community that anyone who has overcome the challenge can join. If you are looking for SOC-provides in Finland, please check csoc.fi.
